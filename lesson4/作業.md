### 7. 台灣有多少人在2020確診?

```sql
SELECT MAX(總確診數) AS 總確診數
FROM world
WHERE 國家 = '台灣' AND 日期 BETWEEN '2020-01-01' AND '2020-12-31';
```

### 17. 哪一日死亡人數最多?

```sql
SELECT 日期, SUM(新增死亡數) AS total_new_deaths
FROM world
GROUP BY 日期
ORDER BY total_new_deaths DESC
LIMIT 1;
```
